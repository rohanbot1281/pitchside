# 2026 Tournament Format

## Expanded 48-team format
The 2026 World Cup is the first with 48 teams, up from 32, playing 104
matches across the United States, Mexico, and Canada from June 11 to
July 19, 2026. Teams are split into 12 groups (A through L) of four.
Each team plays the other three in its group once.

## Advancement rules
The top two teams in each group advance, plus the eight best third-placed
teams, producing a new round of 32. Third-placed teams are ranked by
points, then goal difference, then goals scored, then team conduct score,
then FIFA ranking.

## Knockout schedule
Round of 32 begins June 28. Round of 16 follows, then quarterfinals
(July 9-11), semifinals (July 14-15), the third-place match, and the
final on Sunday, July 19, 2026 at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford,
New Jersey.

## Group stage tiebreakers
Group position is decided by points, then goal difference, then goals
scored, then head-to-head results among tied teams, then fair play
points, then FIFA ranking.
