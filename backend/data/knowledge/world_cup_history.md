# World Cup History

## Champions by tournament
2022 Argentina (beat France on penalties in Qatar), 2018 France,
2014 Germany, 2010 Spain, 2006 Italy, 2002 Brazil, 1998 France,
1994 Brazil, 1990 West Germany, 1986 Argentina, 1982 Italy,
1978 Argentina, 1974 West Germany, 1970 Brazil, 1966 England,
1962 Brazil, 1958 Brazil, 1954 West Germany, 1950 Uruguay,
1938 Italy, 1934 Italy, 1930 Uruguay.

## Titles by country
Brazil 5, Germany 4, Italy 4, Argentina 3, France 2, Uruguay 2,
England 1, Spain 1.

## Notable records
Brazil is the only nation to play in every World Cup. Lionel Messi
holds the record for most World Cup appearances by an outfield player
and captained Argentina to the 2022 title. Miroslav Klose holds the
all-time scoring record with 16 goals. The 2026 edition is the first
hosted by three nations and the first 48-team tournament. The United
States previously hosted in 1994; Mexico in 1970 and 1986, making it
the first country to host three times.

## Debutants in 2026
Cape Verde, Curaçao, Jordan, and Uzbekistan are all appearing at their
first World Cup.
