# 2026 World Cup Groups

The final draw was held December 5, 2025 in Washington, DC. The last six
spots were decided in March 2026 playoffs.

## Group A
Mexico, South Korea, South Africa, Czechia. Czechia qualified through the
UEFA playoffs. Mexico opens the tournament against South Africa at
Estadio Azteca on June 11, 2026.

## Group B
Canada, Switzerland, Qatar, Bosnia and Herzegovina. Bosnia and Herzegovina
came through the UEFA playoffs.

## Group C
Brazil, Morocco, Scotland, Haiti. Haiti is appearing at its first World Cup
since 1974.

## Group D
United States, Paraguay, Australia, Türkiye. Türkiye beat Kosovo in the
UEFA playoff final to claim the last spot. The USMNT opens against
Paraguay on June 12 at SoFi Stadium.

## Group E
Germany, Ecuador, Ivory Coast, Curaçao. Curaçao is the smallest nation
ever to qualify for a World Cup.

## Group F
Netherlands, Japan, Tunisia, Sweden. Sweden came through the UEFA playoffs.

## Group G
Belgium, Iran, Egypt, New Zealand.

## Group H
Spain, Uruguay, Saudi Arabia, Cape Verde. Cape Verde is a World Cup debutant.

## Group I
France, Senegal, Norway, Iraq. Iraq won inter-confederation playoff
Pathway 2 in March 2026 — its first World Cup since 1986. Norway, led by
Erling Haaland, is at its first World Cup since 1998.

## Group J
Argentina, Austria, Algeria, Jordan. Argentina enters as defending
champion. Jordan is a debutant.

## Group K
Portugal, Colombia, Uzbekistan, DR Congo. Uzbekistan is a debutant.
DR Congo won inter-confederation playoff Pathway 1 — its first World Cup
since 1974 (as Zaire).

## Group L
England, Croatia, Panama, Ghana.
